EPSA Election Rules

Phase I: University Representatives The candidate with the highest number of votes in each university automatically becomes the official EPSA representative for that campus. To vote in Phase I, you must be a verified student of that university.
Phase II: National Executive Board (NEB) All university representatives and qualified candidates enter a unified national ballot.
  • 1st Place: President
  • 2nd Place: Vice President
  • 3rd Place: Secretary General
The remaining positions (Directors) are subsequently assigned based on competence and portfolio review by the established leadership hierarchy.
